Vanessa MacIsaac            

Hi there!

I'm Vanessa, welcome to my website! I recently completed my degree in Computer Science, with an option in Bioinformatics at the University of Ottawa.

I love developing mobile applications, travel photography, astronomy and origami. Sports are also a big interest of mine, I'm a fan of the Ottawa Senators and the New England Patriots.

I am very passionate about volunteering and helping others. One of my main focuses is encouraging young women to pursue a career in technology.
